Ingredients:
     - 1 beef brisket (2-3 lbs)
     - 2 Tbs yellow mustard
     - 1 packet powdered onion soup mix
     - crockpot

Directions:
     1. Place brisket in the crockpot with the fatty side up.
     2. Spread the mustard on the top of the brisket.
     3. Sprinkle onion soup mix over the meat.
     4. Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours.
     5. Serve & enjoy!
